Vendor Terms and Conditions

1. Introduction

Welcome to ShopEase's Vendor Program. These Terms and Conditions ("Terms") govern your participation as a vendor on the ShopEase platform. By registering as a vendor, you agree to comply with these Terms.

2. Vendor Eligibility

To become a vendor on ShopEase, you must:

  • Be at least 18 years of age
  • Have a valid business registration or license
  • Provide accurate business and contact information
  • Maintain a valid bank account for receiving payments
  • Comply with all applicable laws and regulations

3. Product Guidelines

As a vendor, you are responsible for:

  • Ensuring all products meet quality and safety standards
  • Providing accurate product descriptions, images, and pricing
  • Maintaining sufficient inventory to fulfill orders
  • Not listing prohibited items (as defined in our Prohibited Items Policy)
  • Complying with all intellectual property laws

4. Fees and Payments

ShopEase charges the following fees:

  • Commission: 10% of the product sale price (excluding shipping)
  • Monthly subscription fee: $29.99 (waived for the first 3 months)
  • Payment processing fee: 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction

Payments will be processed and transferred to your account within 14 days after order completion, minus applicable fees.

5. Shipping and Fulfillment

Vendors are responsible for:

  • Processing and shipping orders within 2 business days
  • Providing tracking information for all shipments
  • Setting reasonable shipping rates
  • Ensuring products are properly packaged
  • Handling returns and exchanges according to ShopEase's Return Policy

6. Customer Service

Vendors must:

  • Respond to customer inquiries within 24 hours
  • Resolve customer issues promptly and professionally
  • Maintain a customer satisfaction rating of at least 4.0/5.0
  • Process refunds for valid claims within 3 business days

7. Account Suspension and Termination

ShopEase reserves the right to suspend or terminate vendor accounts for:

  • Violation of these Terms
  • Consistently poor customer service
  • Selling prohibited items
  • Fraudulent activity
  • Failure to maintain minimum performance standards

8. Changes to Terms

ShopEase may update these Terms at any time. Vendors will be notified of significant changes, and continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of the updated Terms.

9. Contact Information

For questions or concerns regarding these Terms, please contact vendor-support@shopease.com.
